Retail Brand

Retail • Sales & Inquiries

Budget Type

Lifetime Budget

Primary Goal

Increase engagement and customer inquiries for a local retail brand using Instagram ads

Strategy & Campaign Setup
Campaign Objective
  • Lead Generation
  • Selected lead forms to allow users to submit inquiries directly through Instagram
Audience Targeting
  • Advantage+ audience
  • Detailed interest-based targeting to improve relevance
Creative Format
  • Video creatives optimized for mobile placements
Funnel Logic
  • Cold Audience: Video ads to build awareness and engagement

Engagement

Video creatives significantly improved engagement, contributing to higher add-to-cart actions and purchases

CTR Performance

Click-through rate improved and performed exceptionally well compared to initial benchmarks

CPC Optimization

Cost per click was reduced through audience refinement and creative testing

Regular creative testing combined with audience optimization helped reduce wasted spend and improve overall campaign efficiency.
